## Assessment

The DDEV + AI ecosystem is **very active** with 15+ plugins/addons found. Key observations:
- Multiple competing Claude Code integrations (FreelyGive official, plus 4+ alternatives)
- Two MCP server integrations for DDEV (codingsasi, AkibaAT)
- Multiple Ollama integrations for local LLM
- Official Drupal AI project has its own DDEV addon (Drupal-AI/ddev-drupal-ai)
- Official DDEV blog documents AI contribution workflows
- Cross-pollination from Laravel AI community (Laravel Boost pattern)

### Recommended for "Standard" list:
1. FreelyGive/ddev-claude-code (official FreelyGive)
2. Drupal-AI/ddev-drupal-ai (Drupal AI module ecosystem)
3. codingsasi/ddev-mcp OR AkibaAT/ddev-mcp (MCP integration)
4. tyler36/ddev-ollama (local LLM)
