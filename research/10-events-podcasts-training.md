## Play to Impact Hackathon — Full Details

- **Location:** The One building, Brussels — EC executive headquarters
- **Dates:** January 27-28, 2026
- **Participants:** 70+ developers, designers, UX specialists
- **Teams:** Nine competing teams
- **Requirement:** All solutions required integration with Mistral AI (European sovereign LLM)
- **Sponsors:** Mistral AI (credits + support), amazee.ai, DrupalForge/DevPanel
- **Winning Team:** #4 "Token Burners" — created FlowDrop Agents + FlowDrop Node Sessions modules
- **Jury:** Tim Lehnen (Drupal Association), Jamie Abrahams (AI Initiative)
- **Organizers:** Sabina La Felice, Monika Vladimirova, Raquel Fialho, Antonio De Marco, Rosa Ordinana-Calabuig
