## DrupalCon & Community Events

### DrupalCon Barcelona 2024
- Dries Buytaert demonstrated new AI capabilities
- AI-enabled Drupal CMS (Starshot) demos
- Introduction of AI module capabilities
- Blog: https://imagexmedia.com/blog/drupal-ai-capabilities-latest-demos

### DrupalCon Atlanta 2025
- Marcus Johansson introduced AI Automators powering Drupal CMS 1.0
- Features: content assistance in CKEditor, Alt text generation, no-code AI Agent creation
- AI Agents vs AI Automators distinction established
- URL: https://pantheon.io/drupalcon (Pantheon coverage)
- Blog: https://www.teamcolab.com/insights/what-we-learned-at-drupalcon-2025-experience-builder-ai-and-the-future-of-drupal/

### DrupalCon Vienna 2025 (October)
- AI sessions were standing room only
- Keynote: AI-enabled visual editing, site templates, autonomous agents, workflow orchestration
- State of Drupal presentation: https://dri.es/state-of-drupal-presentation-october-2025
- Canvas AI Assistant featured
- Blog: https://systemseed.com/insights/why-this-years-drupalcon-felt-like-a-turning-point/
